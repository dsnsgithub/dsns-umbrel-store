Docker Compose Override Applier

What this does: This utility applies docker-compose.override.yml files to their corresponding docker-compose.yml files across all apps in your app-data directory. It creates backups before making changes.
⚠️ Warning: This will modify your docker-compose.yml files. Backups are created automatically, but make sure you understand what overrides you're applying.
